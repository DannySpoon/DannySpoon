Photography



I have been practising photography for around 4 years, ever since I first got a DSLR in 2016. I bought my camera because I wanted to document my Erasmus year abroad in Venice but unfortunately I didn't know what I was doing and ended up just pointing and shooting. I have got a little better since then and have taken photos travelling around central Europe, eastern Asia and the Caribbean. I still have my 4 year old Nikon D3300 and I use either the kit lens or a Sigma 70-200mm.
